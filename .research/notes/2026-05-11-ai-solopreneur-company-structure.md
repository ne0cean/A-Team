## Summary of Key Findings

I've completed deep research across 12+ authoritative sources. Here are the critical insights:

### **The Viable Model (2025-2026)**

A one-person AI company is now **structurally viable** with:
- **Operating costs**: $300-500/month ($3-12K annually) — 95-98% reduction vs. traditional startups
- **Operating margins**: 60-80% vs. 10-20% traditional (economics inverted)
- **Revenue scale proven**: $300K-$3.6M ARR sustained by solo founders
- **Time allocation**: AI handles 80-85% of execution; human focuses on strategy, taste, relationships

### **Real Examples**

1. **Pieter Levels**: $3.5M ARR across portfolio of 70+ products (PHP/jQuery stack)
2. **Danny Postma**: $3.6M ARR from HeadshotPro (AI headshot generator)
3. **Marc Lou**: $1.032M in 2025 from 3 SaaS products (80% code via AI)
4. **Maor Shlomo (Base44)**: $80M Wix acquisition in 6 months, 350K users, bootstrapped
5. **Tatsuya Mizuno**: 3 blogs, 24 APIs, 10 extensions, 35 products — $60-80/month AI spend
6. **Justin Welsh**: $3.8M ARR knowledge business (audience → courses → sponsorships)

### **Essential Departments & Tools**

| Department | Manual | Automated | Tools | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Engineering** | Architecture, code review, taste | 80% implementation | Claude Code, Cursor | $20-100 |
| **Marketing** | Strategy, brand voice | Content generation, posting | ChatGPT, Descript, Canva AI | $50-200 |
| **Sales** | Deal closure, relationships | Lead qualification, outreach | Intercom Fin, ActiveCampaign, AI agents | $50-150 |
| **Support** | Complex escalations | 80%+ routine tickets | Intercom + AI, Zendesk | $0-100 |
| **Design** | Direction, taste | Asset generation | Canva AI, Midjourney | $10-50 |
| **Ops/Admin** | Workflow design | 95% routine tasks | Zapier, Make, n8n | $50-200 |

### **Sam Altman's Vision**

Three essential roles for a one-person company:
1. **Numbers person** (finance/metrics) — handled by AI (QuickBooks, Wave)
2. **Words person** (communication/marketing) — handled by AI (Claude, ChatGPT)
3. **Visionary/taste** (strategy, design, relationships) — remains fundamentally human

AI can't close major deals or make taste decisions; humans can't code fast or write 100 blog posts/month.

### **Key Skill: AI Orchestration**

Every successful solo founder emphasizes: *the skill that separates winners is knowing which tasks to delegate to agents vs. which require human judgment.* This is now the critical differentiator.

### **Market Validation**

- Solo founders: 23.7% of startups (2019) → 36.3% (mid-2025)
- Zoom's inaugural Solopreneur 50 cohort signals mainstream adoption
- Dario Amodei (Anthropic): 70-80% probability of $1B one-person company in 2026

### **Risks & Limits**

- Burnout from wearing all hats (psychological sustainability)
- Quality control depends entirely on human taste
- Customer relationship depth may suffer from automation
- Hiring inflection point likely at $2-3M ARR
