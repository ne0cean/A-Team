## Key Takeaways

**The most authoritative complete organizational taxonomy is APQC's Process Classification Framework v8.0**, with 13 High-Level Categories that cover 100% of business functions across any industry:

**Operating Processes (6):**
1. Develop Vision & Strategy
2. Develop & Manage Products/Services
3. Market & Sell
4. Manage Supply Chain
5. Deliver Services
6. Manage Customer Service

**Management & Support (7):**
7. Develop & Manage Human Capital
8. Manage IT
9. Manage Financial Resources
10. Acquire/Construct/Manage Assets
11. Manage Risk/Compliance/Remediation
12. Manage External Relationships
13. Develop & Manage Business Capabilities

**Cross-cutting with Porter's Value Chain reveals:**
- **Primary (Revenue-generating):** Marketing/Sales, Product Development, Service Delivery, Operations
- **Support (Cost Centers):** Finance, HR, IT, Legal, Admin, Procurement
- **Strategic Control:** Risk/Compliance, Governance, Strategy, ESG

**Modern twist (McKinsey 2026):** Organizations are shifting from functional silos to **flow-based structures orchestrating humans + AI**, especially in shared services (Finance, HR) and customer-facing functions.
